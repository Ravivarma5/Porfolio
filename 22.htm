Temperature Unit Converter

This versatile temperature converter is expertly designed to facilitate conversions between Celsius (C), Fahrenheit (F), and Kelvin (K) in any combination desired. Users can easily select their preferred units for both the input and output from a user-friendly interface. Upon choosing the units and entering the numerical value in the designated box, the converter promptly calculates and displays the equivalent temperature in the selected unit. This tool is ideal for educational, scientific, and everyday purposes, offering quick and accurate temperature conversions with minimal effort.
